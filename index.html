Welcome to the East Asian Language Sharing Association!

Welcome to EALSA, the East Asian Language Sharing Association! We are a language club based at the University of Arizona dedicated to helping individuals practice and improve their Japanese, Korean, and Chinese language skills. Our club meets weekly through Zoom, making it accessible to anyone who wishes to join and practice their language skills from the comfort of their own home.


Our main goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where language learners of all levels can practice speaking with other like-minded individuals. We believe that the best way to improve language skills is through conversation, and our club provides a space for individuals to practice and improve their speaking skills in a supportive and encouraging community.


Whether you are a beginner or an advanced language learner, EALSA welcomes everyone who is interested in practicing their Japanese, Korean, or Chinese language skills. We strive to provide a fun and engaging way for individuals to immerse themselves in East Asian cultures through language learning and cultural exchange.


Join us today and start your language learning journey with EALSA!
